featured services

Custom Tooling & Molding

Vision Packaging Australia provides complete custom moulding services, from the initial concept through to moulding and supply of finished product.

We will discuss and assist with your design, supply full detailed drafted drawings for sign-off, then proceed to actual building of the tooling for trial runs before proceeding to final production.

Artwork Design

Vision Packaging is able to provide complete graphic design facilities for all budgets. We sit down to discuss and understand your product and target market, the image you want to portray and then present you with a number of design options.

This process will then be fine-tuned and repeated, until the ideal package is created for your brand.

Decorating Services

Vision Packaging provides full UV screen printing, off-set printing and hot foil stamping services directly onto our complete range of packaging products.

Labeling and label application services are also provided for when this is the better choice, or when direct screen printing is not an option.

Point of Sale Merchandise

With our local and off-shore production facilities we will ensure, that the best balance between price, efficiency and timing of your project is achieved.
